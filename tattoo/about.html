Our Tattoos Last Longer Than Most Marriages.

Tattoos are an expression of individuality, “Tattoos are for a Lifetime”. Tribal Tattoo Shop was established in 2007, We are the ones who set the trend of Tattoo in Mangalore, when tattoos were only limited to big cities. Our artists are well trained as they have been trained from the well known and experienced artists from Goa and Nepal. Our artists are good at free hand drawing and they are all from the art background. We believe tattoo is a permanent piece of priceless art. Clients come to our shop with a picture or design in mind or they consult us to create the desired image.
